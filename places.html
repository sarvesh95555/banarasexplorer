Popular Places to Visit

Discover the most famous places in Banaras known for their spirituality and historical significance.

Kashi Vishwanath Temple

A famous temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas.

Dashashwamedh Ghat

The main ghat for the daily Ganga Aarti, known for its vibrant atmosphere.

Sarnath

A significant Buddhist pilgrimage site where Buddha gave his first sermon.
